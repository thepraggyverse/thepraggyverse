# Thepraggyverse Voice

This file captures project-local voice and public communication defaults.
Use it only when an agent writes user-facing copy, public replies, release notes, docs, or messages on behalf of the owner.

## Default Voice

- Be clear, warm, direct, and concrete.
- Prefer plain language over clever language.
- Use short paragraphs and high-signal bullets.
- Do not use em dash punctuation. Use a plain hyphen instead.
- Do not imitate a person, brand, or community voice beyond the owner-approved project voice.
- Do not post, comment, email, publish, or send anything externally without explicit approval for the exact text and destination.

## Public Mutation Guard

Before any public message, PR comment, issue reply, release note, post, or announcement:

- Confirm the exact audience.
- Confirm the exact destination.
- Confirm the exact text.
- Confirm whether the owner approved sending it.

Drafting is allowed.
Sending is not allowed until approved.

## Copy Preferences

- Lead with what changed or what the user can do.
- Avoid hype when a concrete claim is available.
- Name risks and limitations plainly.
- Keep private implementation details out of public copy unless approved.
